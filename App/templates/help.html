These type of users are the lab staff and the teaching staff that will be able to make requests for the stationery items.

•Making a request:
1. Click on “Make a request” option either from the home page or from the Nav bar.To access items by category, click on “Categories” and to access all the items at once,click on “See all Items”.
2. Select the required category and search for the items required.
3. Select the item required by clicking on “Request” button.
4. Add the necessary item details.
5. Click on “Confirm Request” to confirm.
•Requests Log:
Once you have selected all the required items,click on “Requests Log” from the nav bar to view the summary of items requested.
•Managing Profile:
• You can edit your profile details or reset your account password by clicking on your profile on the navbar.

•Sanctioning Requests:
1. To check for the users requests, click on “Requests” option on the navbar or from the home page.
2. To accept a user’s request, click on “Send” and to reject the request, click on “Delete”. Before accepting or deleting a request ,you can write a comment.
3. After accepting of deleting a request, you can check all the requests status by clicking on the “Requests Log” button.
•Adding New Items/Categories of Items:
1. Click on “Stock-->1.Categories 2.See all items”.
2. To add a new category, select “Categories”.
3. Click on “Add category”.
4. Add Category name and choose a picture file to upload for the category.
5. To add a new item, select “See all items”.
6. Click on “Add a new item”.
•Editing Existing Items/Categories of Items:
To edit an item, click on the “Edit” option to manage all the following parameters:
•Item Name
•Quantity
•Required Quantity
•Category
•Maximum Limit
•Minimum Limit
•Quota
•Admin Summary:
To view the summary of the requests accepted or rejected, click on “Admin Summary”.
•Managing Profile:
• You can edit your profile details or reset your account password by clicking on your profile on the navbar.
